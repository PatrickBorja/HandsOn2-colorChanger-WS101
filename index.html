All ABOUT EYES!

DID YOU KNOW?

Blue Eyes: Genetic trait resulting from a lack of melanin;
more common in people of European descent.

Approximately 8-10% of the global population has blue eyes,
with a higher prevalence in people of European descent.

Green Eyes: Result from a mix of blue and yellow pigments; relatively rare,
associated with mystery and more common in those with Celtic or Germanic ancestry.

Green eyes are rarer, constituting about 1-2% of the world's population,
more commonly found in individuals with Celtic or Germanic ancestry.
